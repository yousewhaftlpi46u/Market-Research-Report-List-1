<p><p>The Rhenium Sputtering Target market is divided into two primary types: rotary and non-rotatable targets. Rotary targets are designed for continuous processing, allowing for efficient material deposition in high-volume applications. They feature a cylindrical shape that enables uniform sputtering over a larger surface area. In contrast, non-rotatable targets are typically flat and used for smaller-scale or specialized applications. These targets may offer cost advantages but can result in less uniform deposition, making them suitable for specific needs.</p></p>
